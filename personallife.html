Personal Life of Francis Crick...

Crick married twice, fathered three children and was the grandfather of six grandchildren; his brother Anthony (born in 1918) predeceased him in 1966.

  1. Spouses:

    • Ruth Doreen Crick, née Dodd (b. 1913, m. 18th February 1940 – 8th May 1947. d. 2011), became Mrs. James Stewart Potter
    • Odile Crick, née Speed (b. 11th August 1920, m. 14th August 1949 – 28 July 2004, d. 5th July 2007)

  2. Children:

    • Michael Francis Compton (b. 25th November 1940) [by Doreen Crick]
    • Gabrielle Anne (b. 15th July 1951)

  3. Grandchildren

    • Alexander (b. March 1974)
    • Kindra (b. May 1976)
    • Camberley (b. June 1978)
    • Francis Henry Riley (b. February 1981), Michael & Barbara Crick's four children
    • Mark & Nicholas, the late Jacqueline and Christopher Nichols' children

Crick died of colon cancer on the morning of 28th July 2004 at the University of California, San Diego (UCSD) Thornton Hospital in La Jolla; he was cremated and his ashes were scattered into the Pacific Ocean. A public memorial was held on 27th September 2004 at the Salk Institute, La Jolla, near San Diego, California; guest speakers included James Watson, Sydney Brenner, Alex Rich, Seymour Benzer, Aaron Klug, Christof Koch, Pat Churchland, Vilayanur Ramachandran, Tomaso Poggio, Leslie Orgel, Terry Sejnowski, his son Michael Crick, and his youngest daughter Jacqueline Nichols. A private memorial for family and colleagues was held on 3rd August 2004.
